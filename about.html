About Us

The major profile of Our Company is buying vehicles at Japanese Car Auctions and further exporting throughout the world.

Over 50,000 vehicles are sold daily at 90 auctions across Japan. Indeed an impressive choice. We will be able to buy the vehicle which meets your expectations, provide full service for export documentation, transportation and shipment it as agreed with the customers to any country in the world a.s.a.p.
